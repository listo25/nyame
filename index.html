HTML, or HyperText Markup Language, is the foundation of every website. It’s the standard language used to create web pages, and it’s essential for anyone looking to build a website. HTML allows you to structure your content with elements like headings, paragraphs, images, links, and more. Even though it might seem complex at first, HTML is easy to learn, and with just a few basic tags, you can create your first web page! In this section, we’ll guide you through the essential HTML elements and help you understand how they work together to form a well-structured website.
